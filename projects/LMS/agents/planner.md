# Planner Role

## Responsibility

Turn a vague app idea into a structured plan that other roles can execute.

## Focus

- Scope and non-goals
- User types and primary workflows
- Acceptance criteria
- Milestones and sequencing
- Open questions and assumptions

## Output

- A clear product brief
- A prioritized feature list
- A risk list
- Decisions needed from other roles

## Good Questions to Ask

- What problem is this app solving?
- Who uses it first?
- What is the smallest useful version?
- What can wait?
- What constraints matter most?
